About

Hello! I am Norbu, a Tibetan born in an occupied country (Tibet), raised as a stateless in India, a refugee in France, and currently a French citizen living in Paris.
The fusion of art and technology has always fascinated me and I have never been afraid to take the plunge.

As a photographer, I like to capture the moment by appreciating the beauty and value of what I see.
As a visual designer and web developer, I love creating beautiful visual and web experiences focused on simplicity and purpose on the digital platform and in life. I love creating designing experiences that help people to reach their goal.
Since data takes on a new importance and value in the digital age, currently I am in process of becoming a Data Analyst.

I am always open to learning and improving my skills and myself, as there is no end to learning, through learning we grow.

One of my favorite quotes is from Tenzin Gyatso, His Holiness the 14th Dalai Lama of Tibet:

“If you want others to be happy, practice compassion.
If you want to be happy, practice compassion.”
