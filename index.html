About

Hello! I am Norbu, a Tibetan born in Tibet, raised as a stateless in India, a refugee in France, and currently a French citizen living in Paris. The fusion of art and technology has always fascinated me and I have never been afraid to take the plunge, be it with Paint, Photoshop, Lightroom, Figma, or Adobe XD.

As a photographer, I like to capture the moment by appreciating the beauty and value of what I see.

Recently, I have taken up creative digital design and web development. I love creating beautiful web experiences focused on simplicity and purpose on the internet and in life. I am always open to learning and improving my skills and myself, as there is no end to learning, but there are many beginnings.

One of my favorite quotes is from Tenzin Gyatso, the 14th Dalai Lama of Tibet:
“If you want others to be happy, practice compassion. If you want to be happy, practice compassion.”
